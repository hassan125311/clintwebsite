What is Organic Agriculture?

Organic agriculture is a farming system that promotes ecological balance, biodiversity, and sustainability through the use of natural methods and materials.

Key Principles of Organic Farming

Soil Health

Maintaining and enhancing soil fertility through composting and crop rotation.

Biodiversity

Encouraging a diverse range of plants and animals to promote ecosystem balance.

Sustainable Practices

Utilizing methods that reduce environmental impact resources.
